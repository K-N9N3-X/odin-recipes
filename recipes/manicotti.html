Ingredients

    Ricotta Cheese
    Spinach
    Jumbo Shells Noodles
    Pasta Sauce of your choosing

Steps

    1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
    2. Boil Jumbo Shell noodles.
    3. Mix filling for the jumbo Shells (Spinach and Ricotta).
    4. Remove boiled noodles from pot and fill with filling.
    5. Placed filled Jumbo Noodles into a Casserole dish and cover with sauce.
    6. place Casserole dish into the oven and bake at 350 degrees for 30 - 45 minutes.
